#  Business Intelligence Dashboard using Power BI

An interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product insights from the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, identify high-performing products and regions, evaluate profitability, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## Key Business Insights

- West region generated the highest revenue.
- Technology category contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Phones and Chairs were the highest revenue-generating products.
- Furniture showed comparatively lower profitability.
- Consumer segment contributed more than half of the total revenue.
- Only a few products generated losses, indicating strong overall profitability.
